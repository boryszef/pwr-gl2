Welcome to Show YourShelf!


The application which allows you to compare your thoughts about YourShelf (I mean yourselfe) (Self-assesment in menu), your personality, confirmed by Personality Test (created by our lovely psychologist) and what is in your genes, from Genomelink. Connect Show YourShelf with Genomelink to obtain all features.

We give you 2 ways of presentation of your results - radar, which looks like spider's web (Results in menu) or by progress bars which show your "progress" in particular traits (Results bars in menu).

What about traits? There are 5 aviable traits - Big Five of traits: agreeableness, conscientiousness, extraversion, neuroticism and openness. You can read about each of them after choosing "Personality test" and choosing a trait.

Invate friends to use this applications, then you can add them in YourShelf where you can search for them, or accept their invitation in Friend Requests

With friends you can try to "walk in their's shoes" and make a test one more time answering as you they would answer. Then you can check if you were close to what is in their genes.

You can as well send request to friends to remind them to make your test, and see what they think about you!
